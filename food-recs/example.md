## ☕ Coffee
- **G&B Coffee** — Inside Grand Central Market. The horchata latte is something special. Always a line, worth every minute.
- **Intelligentsia Coffee** — Silver Lake location has the best vibe. Get a pour-over and sit outside.

## 🍳 Breakfast
- **Sqirl** — The ricotta toast with seasonal jam is the move. Get there before 9am or the line gets brutal.
- **Eggslut** — Order the Slut: a coddled egg over potato purée served in a jar. Sounds weird, tastes incredible.

## 🍜 Lunch
- **Howlin' Ray's** — Nashville hot chicken in Chinatown. The "hot" and "country hot" are very different things. Start humble.
- **Langer's Deli** — Widely considered the best pastrami sandwich in the country. Order the #19 (pastrami, Swiss, coleslaw on rye).

## 🍕 Dinner
- **Mozza** — Nancy Silverton's spot. The burrata with seasonal accompaniment is always perfect. Reservations required.
- **Night + Market** — Thai food in WeHo. The boat noodle soup and northern sausage are must-orders.

## 🍰 Dessert
- **Salt & Straw** — Rotating seasonal flavors. The honey lavender is a classic. Don't skip it.
